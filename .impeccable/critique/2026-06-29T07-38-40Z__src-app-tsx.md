## Overall Impression

The interface presents a compelling corporate presence. It successfully avoids standard light SaaS cream presets in favor of an authoritative BUMN Dark Navy system. However, the presence of text gradients and uppercase kicker eyebrows makes it feel slightly templated. Replacing these tells with cleaner, typography-driven details will elevate it to a premium tier.

## What's Working
- **Color Strategy**: The use of Deep Navy (`#0d1e3d`) and Ink Navy (`#0b0f19`) provides a solid, serious container for compliance topics, matching the brand guidelines.
- **Clear Information Architecture**: The flow from GRC services -> client targets -> self-assessment -> lead intake feels natural and logical.

## Priority Issues

- **[P1] Gradient Text Slop Tell**
  - **Why it matters**: Multi-color text gradients look decorative rather than authoritative, degrading the professional trust required by BUMN/Banking clients.
  - **Fix**: Replace the gradients in [Hero.tsx](file:///C:/backup/Daya-Solusi-Integra/src/components/Hero.tsx) (lines 38, 149) with a solid color, such as BUMN Gold (`#cca43b`) or stark white, emphasizing through weight.
  - **Suggested command**: `$impeccable quieter src/components/Hero.tsx`

- **[P1] Browser alerts and lack of inline error validation**
  - **Why it matters**: Blocking browser alerts (`alert()`) break user flow and feel amateurish, while raw native inputs do not guide users to recovery.
  - **Fix**: Remove the raw browser alert in [Assessment.tsx](file:///C:/backup/Daya-Solusi-Integra/src/components/Assessment.tsx) (line 40) and replace it with a styled inline alert/warning container under the organization input field.
  - **Suggested command**: `$impeccable harden src/components/Assessment.tsx`

- **[P2] Tracked Uppercase Kicker Eyebrows**
  - **Why it matters**: Repeating all-caps widely-spaced kicker labels ("Interactive Tool", "Hubungi Konsultan Kami") is an AI scaffolding signature that clutters the visual hierarchy.
  - **Fix**: Remove these kicker badges and replace them with a visual indicator (like a clean solid line accent) or a clean lowercase subheader.
  - **Suggested command**: `$impeccable typeset src/components/Assessment.tsx`

- **[P2] Lack of keyboard/ARIA accessibility for interactive elements**
  - **Why it matters**: Compliance officers and GRC auditors expect accessible tools. The custom radio cards and drawers lack focus states and keyboard nav.
  - **Fix**: Implement full keyboard event listeners (`onKeyDown`), `tabIndex`, and aria role attributes on the custom options in [Assessment.tsx](file:///C:/backup/Daya-Solusi-Integra/src/components/Assessment.tsx).
  - **Suggested command**: `$impeccable audit src/components/Assessment.tsx`
