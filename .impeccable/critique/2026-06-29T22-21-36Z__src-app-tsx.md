## Anti-Patterns Verdict

*   **LLM Assessment**: The interface has several classic AI layout tells. It overuses tiny tracked uppercase monospaced headers (`font-mono text-xs uppercase`) above multiple sections. It also violates the border/shadow styling ban by combining thin 1px borders with large drop shadows (`shadow-2xl`) on cards and drawers, and uses over-rounded capsules (`rounded-full`) for navigation buttons. A left-stripe accent border (`border-l border-blue-500/30 pl-4`) in the Hero section violates the side-stripe ban.
*   **Deterministic Scan**: The automated detector successfully scanned the project files and flagged **1 warning** in `src/index.css` (line 1):
    *   **Rule**: `overused-font` (Inter) — Google Fonts: Inter is a highly overused face that can detract from a unique and distinctive brand personality.
*   **Visual Overlays**: No visual overlays injected. Skipped browser-side visualization because visual testing is not supported in the current headless CLI execution environment.

## Overall Impression

The landing page captures the sovereign corporate consulting look extremely well through the deep Ink Navy and BUMN Blue layout. However, it suffers from minor visual noise, mixed casing/language tags, and several subtle AI tells (floating chat badge, thin borders with massive shadows, and side-stripe accents) that weaken its executive authority.

## What's Working

1.  **Audience-Aligned Copy**: The copy leverages authoritative Indonesian regulatory terms (BPK audits, GCG, ICOFR, POJK), which immediately builds trust with BUMN compliance officers.
2.  **Sophisticated Dark Mode**: The canvas uses a rich Ink Navy (#0b0f19) background with clean slate-slate borders that feel highly professional.
3.  **Keyboard Accessibility**: Supporting keys 1-4 for wizard choices and `Ctrl + /` for the advisor drawer shows exceptional technical polish.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Violation of Border + Shadow Constraint (Visual Quality)
*   **Why it matters**: Mixing thin borders and massive shadows muddy the dark mode and violate the Flat-By-Rest visual rules.
*   **Fix**: Remove `shadow-2xl` and large shadows from cards and panels at rest. Let them remain flat.
*   **Suggested command**: `$impeccable layout` or edit `src/index.css` and individual component files to clean up drop-shadow classes.

### [P1] AI Tell - Floating Chat Widget (Brand Perception)
*   **Why it matters**: A floating live chat bubble feels like standard consumer SaaS and dilutes BUMN executive authority.
*   **Fix**: Remove the floating chat badge and integrate the AI Consultation trigger strictly inside the Header and Hero CTA blocks.
*   **Suggested command**: `$impeccable distill` or edit `src/App.tsx`.

### [P2] Language and Casing Inconsistencies (Polish)
*   **Why it matters**: Mixing English labels ("ITGC Orchestrated") with Indonesian text ("Lingkungan Pengendalian") reads like unedited template scaffolding.
*   **Fix**: Translate all visual tags, labels, and charts into formal Indonesian equivalents.
*   **Suggested command**: `$impeccable typeset` or edit `src/components/Hero.tsx` and `src/components/Assessment.tsx`.

### [P3] Personal Email Validation Block (Conversion Rate)
*   **Why it matters**: Rejecting all non-corporate domains locks out legitimate regional BUMD officers who do not have custom email domains yet.
*   **Fix**: Downgrade the corporate domain error to a soft-warning notification while permitting form submission.
*   **Suggested command**: `$impeccable clarify` or edit `src/components/Contact.tsx`.
