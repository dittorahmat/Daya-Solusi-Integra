## Anti-Patterns & AI Slop Verdict

* **LLM Assessment**: The page feels highly professional, polished, and tailored, avoiding generic templates. It utilizes structural geometry and appropriate corporate styling. However, using **Space Grotesk** and **Inter** is a classic AI template tell (though preserved here for brand identity consistency). The chatbot welcoming text style also carries slight AI tells.
* **Reflex Checks**: The branding register is clean. There are no sketchy SVGs, over-rounded card layouts (corners top out at 16px), or side-stripe borders. Glassmorphism is used tastefully and only where appropriate.

---

## Cognitive Load & Working Memory

* **Checklist Failures (1/8)**: Item 5 (One thing at a time) fails on the results screen, where the user is presented with total score, percentage, Level definition, COSO category averages, AI report CTA, restart button, and print button simultaneously.
* **Working Memory Limit (Cowan's Rule)**: The results view contains 7 distinct options and data points, pushing past the Cowan capacity of 4 items. In contrast, the question wizard successfully limits choices (exactly 4 options per question).

---

## Emotional Journey

* **Peak-End Rule**: Completion of the assessment is a high-satisfaction peak. The lead form pre-fill ensures a seamless end.
* **Valleys**: Entering the corporate name before viewing questions is a high-friction valley. The AI report generation has an artificial client-side delay of ~12.5 seconds, which creates a frustrating waiting loop.
* **Trust Indicators**: Standard phone numbers, physical address, and a dedicated confidentiality badge reassure risk-averse executives.

## Persona Red Flags

### Alex (Impatient Power User)
* **Red Flag**: Forced to wait 12.5 seconds for AI report generation due to fake client-side delay.
* **Red Flag**: Keyboard shortcuts are completely undocumented, forcing manual mouse navigation.

### Jordan (First-Timer)
* **Red Flag**: May miss glossary definitions if they do not hover over dotted-underlined terms.
* **Red Flag**: Choice overload on the assessment results page makes it hard to identify the next step.

### BUMN Director (Project-Specific)
* **Red Flag**: High-friction gate: Must type their official BUMN organization name before they can preview the assessment questions.
* **Red Flag**: No way to export a clean PDF document with executive branding for board presentations.

## What's Working
1. **Exceptional Craft**: Authoritative dark layout with crisp contrast and fluid animations.
2. **State Preservation**: Excellent use of `sessionStorage` in `Assessment` and `AiAdvisor` to prevent data loss.
3. **Synergetic Conversion**: Pre-filling the lead form after the assessment lowers form abandonment rate.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Mandatory Company Name Gating
* **Why it matters**: Executives are highly protective of company data and will abandon the assessment if forced to declare their organization before seeing the questions.
* **Fix**: Allow anonymous assessments or prompt for company/sector details at the end to customize the final report.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable shape`

### [P1] Locked Assessment State (No Edit Option)
* **Why it matters**: If a user completes the assessment and spots a typo or wants to change an answer, they cannot return to the wizard. Wiping everything with "Reset" is too punishing.
* **Fix**: Add an "Edit Answers" button on the results screen that returns the user to the question wizard while preserving their selections.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable polish`

### [P2] Artificial Generation Delay
* **Why it matters**: The forced delay in `generateAiReport` wastes executive time and hurts perceived application performance.
* **Fix**: Resolve the report instantly when the API returns the result.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable optimize`

### [P2] Undocumented Keyboard Shortcuts
* **Why it matters**: Useful accelerators (keys 1-4 and Ctrl+/) are hidden, missing an opportunity for keyboard accessibility and speed.
* **Fix**: Add small visual keyboard shortcuts indicators in the UI.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable audit`
