#### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM Assessment**: The interface displays a highly professional visual structure that adheres perfectly to the "Sovereign Lighthouse" creative north star. It correctly rejects typical SaaS cream-colored backgrounds and neon hacker templates, anchoring the design in a stable Ink Navy (`#0b0f19`). The elements are rectilinear, thin borders (1px) are kept clean and shadowless at rest, and card corners correctly respect the 12px-16px constraints.

**Deterministic Scan**: The automated design detector returned 1 raw violation regarding an overused font (Inter) in `src/index.css`, which was correctly filtered out as a false positive since it is the explicitly designated body font per `DESIGN.md`. All other parameters are clean.

**Visual Overlays**: No visual overlay is available as browser mutable script injection was skipped in this headless evaluation context.

#### Overall Impression
A highly polished, risk-conscious dark interface tailored perfectly to Indonesian enterprise compliance (BUMN/banking). The core assessment flow is engaging and interactive, but the visual delivery of results creates high cognitive noise, and the upfront collection of company names risks early user abandonment.

#### What's Working
1. **Accelerated Keyboard Navigation**: Providing keys 1-4 for option selecting and arrow keys for navigation inside the assessment wizard makes evaluation extremely fast.
2. **Glossary Integration**: Inline definitions on key acronyms prevent terminological confusion for non-expert stakeholders.
3. **Restrained Visual Polish**: Tonal navy layering and subtle glow effects on hover convey premium governance standards.

#### Priority Issues

##### 1. **[P1] Missing Privacy Reassurance on Assessment Start**
* **Why**: Risk management directors at state-owned enterprises (BUMN) will abandon the self-assessment if forced to type their corporate name alongside compliance scores without immediate data-safety guarantees.
* **Fix**: Add a secure-handling note ("Data diproses secara aman dalam memori & dilindungi standar NDA") directly below the Company Name input on Step -1.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable clarify`

##### 2. **[P2] Visual Overload on Assessment Results View**
* **Why**: Presenting multiple metrics, dimension charts, a long AI advisory text block, and the contact form in a single scrolling layout causes severe cognitive fatigue.
* **Fix**: Tab-index the results wrapper (e.g., Tab 1: Ringkasan Skor, Tab 2: Laporan AI) to structure disclosures and separate the contact form visually.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable layout`

##### 3. **[P2] Mobile Glossary Tooltip Interaction Friction**
* **Why**: Tooltips rely on mouse-hover states, which trigger clumsy focus behaviors on touch viewports and fail to close cleanly.
* **Fix**: Incorporate click-outside event listeners to close active tooltips on touch and mobile devices.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable adapt`

##### 4. **[P3] Lack of Report Action Variety (Copy to Clipboard)**
* **Why**: Users are limited to `window.print()`. There is no quick action to copy the advisory findings text for internal slides or emails.
* **Fix**: Add a "Salin Laporan" (Copy to Clipboard) button next to the print command.
* **Suggested Command**: `$impeccable delight`

#### Persona Red Flags

* **Primary Action**: Speed-running the self-assessment to view output quality.
* **Red Flag**: If Alex misclicks an option, there is no backspace/left-arrow key shortcut to quickly undo or step back a page, forcing them to break flow and click the mouse.

##### Predefined Persona: Jordan (First-Timer)
* **Primary Action**: Landing on the site, reading the services, and starting the quiz.
* **Red Flag**: The acronym hover triggers do not work cleanly on Jordan's tablet viewport, forcing double-taps that clip surrounding content.

##### Project-Specific Persona: Pak Bambang (BUMN Compliance Director)
* **Profile**: Highly risk-averse BUMN executive under intense scrutiny from BPK/BPKP. Value confidentiality above all.
* **Primary Action**: Testing DSI's capability and completing the maturity scorecard.
* **Red Flag**: Step -1's requirement to input their company name before viewing questions triggers an immediate security alarm, leading to high bounce rates before entering the assessment.

#### Minor Observations
* The interface alternates between English and Indonesian in its labels (e.g., *AI GRC Consultant* vs *Coba Asesmen*). Standardizing on Indonesian-first enhances trust for domestic state-owned institutions.
